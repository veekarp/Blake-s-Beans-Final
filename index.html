A little black cat inspired an entire business and in 2021, the central coast’s first cat café was opened. Blake’s Beans is dedicated to providing the experience of kitties and coffee all in one location. Patrons can enjoy beverages and treats while spending some time with our lovely café cats. We are committed to administering the company of felines to all the cat lovers of SLO County.
